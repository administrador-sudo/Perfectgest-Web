1. Who we are

This policy describes how the PerfectGest ContabilGest app ("App"), developed by PerfectGest, handles information when you use it on your Android device.

- App name: PerfectGest ContabilGest

2. Core principle: data on your device

The App is designed to work on your phone even without internet day to day. By default:

- Entries, tax obligations, month-end closing and company data stay on your device.
- We do not automatically send your accounting data to PerfectGest servers.
- We do not use advertising or tracking of what you record.

Sharing occurs when you tap "Generate and share PDF", "Export backup" or "Import backup".

Exception (with your consent): sending the month for accountant review, Google Play subscription validation, digital certificate request and data needed for company opening — see section 5.

3. Data the App may process

3.2 Accountant contract acceptance (electronic adhesion)

Before purchasing accountant plans or add-ons, the app requests explicit acceptance of the Accounting Services Contract (version 2.0). Stored locally:

- Company email from profile
- IP address (one-time lookup via public IP service)
- Date and time of the click
- Acceptance ID, contract version and selected product

This serves as adhesion evidence; we do not generate a PDF or require an A1 certificate from the client in the app.

3.3 Not collected automatically

- GPS location
- Phone contact list
- Ad identifiers
- Browsing history outside the App

4. How we use data

We use data to:

- Record and display the cash book and entries
- Track tax obligations and month-end closing
- Generate PDF and backup when requested
- Validate subscription and deliver contracted services (company opening, accountant review, digital certificate) — with consent

Legal basis (LGPD): contract performance and features you request; consent for cloud upload and additional accounting services.

The App does not sell accounting data.

The app does not enable accountant review, digital certificate or company opening without a plan confirmed on Google Play.

You may revoke consent in Privacy & security. Local cash book continues; cloud services are unavailable until new acceptance.

5.3 Company opening — timeline and fees

When contracted (Com Contador monthly or configurable annual package):

- Official fees (Commercial Registry, city hall, tax authority, etc.) are not included — paid by the client to competent bodies.
- The process may take up to 14 days after complete documentation — subject to third-party deadlines.

Contract details: Terms of Use (version 2.0).
